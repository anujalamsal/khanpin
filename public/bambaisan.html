Bambaisan Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 liters milk
  2. 1 cup sugar (optional)
  3. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Boil the milk in a large pot until it reduces to half its original quantity.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder (if using) to the milk and stir well.
  3. Continue boiling the mixture until it thickens and forms a creamy consistency.
  4. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  5. Serve the Bambaisan warm or at room temperature.
