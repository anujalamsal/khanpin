Chukauni Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 large potatoes, boiled, peeled, and diced
  2. 1 cup yogurt
  3. 1 tablespoon mustard oil
  4. 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  5. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  6. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  7. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  8. Salt to taste
  9. 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  10. 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped

Procedures:

  1. In a bowl, mix boiled and diced potatoes with yogurt.
  2. Heat mustard oil in a pan and add fenugreek seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to the pan. Mix well.
  4. Pour the spice mixture over the potato and yogurt mixture. Mix everything well.
  5. Add chopped green chilies and cilantro. Mix again.
  6. Chill the Chukauni in the refrigerator for about 30 minutes.
  7. Serve cold as a side dish or snack.
