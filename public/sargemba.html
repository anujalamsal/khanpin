Khan Pin

sargemba Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Pig's blood
  2. Yangben (wild lichen)
  3. Rice
  4. Spices
  5. Pig intestines

Procedures:

  1. Mix pig's blood, yangben, rice, and spices together.
  2. Encase the mixture in pig intestines.
  3. Cook the mixture until it is firm and fully cooked.
  4. Serve hot.
