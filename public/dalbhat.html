Dal Bhat Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup lentils
  2. 1 cup rice
  3. 2 tablespoons ghee
  4. 1 onion, chopped
  5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 1 teaspoon turmeric
  7. Salt to taste
  8. Water

Procedures:

  1. Wash lentils and rice thoroughly.
  2. In a pot, heat ghee and saut√© onion and garlic until golden brown.
  3. Add turmeric, lentils, rice, salt, and water. Bring to a boil.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer until lentils and rice are cooked.
  5. Serve hot.
