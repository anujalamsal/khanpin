Newari khaja set Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups beaten rice (chiura)
  2. 200g buffalo meat (sukuti) or any dried meat
  3. 1 cup black-eyed peas (bhatmas)
  4. 1/2 cup boiled potatoes, diced
  5. 1/2 cup pickles (achar)
  6. 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  7. 1/4 cup fresh radish, julienned
  8. 2 tablespoons roasted sesame seeds
  9. 1/4 cup green peas, boiled
  10. 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  11. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  12. 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  13. Salt to taste
  14. Mustard oil for cooking

Procedures:

  1. In a pan, heat mustard oil over medium heat. Add turmeric powder and cumin powder.
  2. Add the buffalo meat (sukuti) and cook until it becomes crispy. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the black-eyed peas and roast them until they turn golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, mix the beaten rice, diced boiled potatoes, fresh radish, green peas, and green chilies.
  5. Add roasted sesame seeds and fresh coriander leaves to the mixture.
  6. Season with salt and mix everything well.
  7. Arrange the mixture on a serving plate.
  8. Add the crispy buffalo meat (sukuti) and roasted black-eyed peas (bhatmas) on top.
  9. Serve the Newari Khaja Set with pickles (achar) on the side.
