Mohi Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup plain yogurt

Procedures:

  1. Take the plain yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Add a little water (around 1/4 to 1/2 cup) to the yogurt and whisk well until smooth.
  3. Continue adding water gradually and whisking until you reach the desired thin consistency.
  4. Your thin curd is ready to be served or used in recipes.
