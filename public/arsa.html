Arsa Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups rice flour
  2. 1 cup water
  3. 1 cup jaggery (or brown sugar), grated
  4. 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  5. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  6. Oil for deep frying

Procedures:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour and water to make a smooth batter. Ensure there are no lumps. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate pan, melt the jaggery with a small amount of water over low heat to make a thick syrup. Allow it to cool slightly.
  3. Add the jaggery syrup to the rice flour batter and mix well to form a consistent batter. Stir in the cardamom powder.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Once the oil is hot, pour a small ladleful of the batter into the oil, shaping it into a small round pancake.
  6. Fry the arsa until they turn golden brown on both sides. Make sure to fry them on medium heat so they cook evenly.
  7. Remove the fried arsa from the oil and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve the arsa warm or at room temperature. Enjoy your delicious Nepali sweet treat!
