Samay baji

Ingredients:

  1. Beaten Rice (Chiura)
  2. Boiled Black Soybeans (Bodi)
  3. Roasted and Ground Soybeans (Bhatmas)
  4. Roasted and Ground Corn (Makai)
  5. Boiled Potatoes (Aloo)
  6. Boiled Black-Eyed Peas (Kerau)
  7. Ginger Paste
  8. Garlic Paste
  9. Green Chili Paste
  10. Yogurt
  11. Mustard Oil
  12. Salt
  13. Turmeric Powder
  14. Coriander Leaves

Procedures:

  1. Mix the beaten rice, boiled black soybeans, roasted and ground soybeans, roasted and ground corn, and boiled potatoes in a bowl.
  2. Add boiled black-eyed peas to the mixture.
  3. Mix in ginger paste, garlic paste, and green chili paste for flavor.
  4. Add yogurt to the mixture and mix well.
  5. Heat mustard oil in a pan and add salt and turmeric powder.
  6. Pour the hot oil over the mixture and mix thoroughly.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  8. Serve the Samay Baji and enjoy!
