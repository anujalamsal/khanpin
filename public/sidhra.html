Sidhra Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup rice flour
  2. 1/2 cup wheat flour
  3. 1/2 cup grated coconut
  4. 1/2 cup jaggery (or sugar)
  5. 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  6. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  7. 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg powder
  8. 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  9. Water, as needed

Procedures:

  1. In a bowl, mix rice flour, wheat flour, grated coconut, jaggery, cardamom powder, nutmeg powder, and baking soda.
  2. Add ghee to the mixture and mix well.
  3. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
  4. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into cylindrical or round shapes.
  5. Steam the shaped dough in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes, or until cooked through.
  6. Remove the steamed sidhra from the steamer and let them cool slightly before serving.
  7. Sidhra can be served as a snack or dessert. Enjoy!
