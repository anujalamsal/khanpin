Mithila thali Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups rice
  2. 1 cup yellow lentils (moong dal)
  3. 4 cups water
  4. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  5. Salt to taste
  6. 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (potato, carrot, peas)
  7. 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  8. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  9. 1 onion, finely chopped
  10. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  11. 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  12. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  13. 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  14. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  15. Fresh cilantro for garnish
  16. Pickles and yogurt (for serving)

Procedures:

  1. Rinse the rice and lentils separately under cold water.
  2. In a pot, add the lentils, turmeric powder, and salt to 2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer until the lentils are soft and cooked through (about 15-20 minutes). Set aside.
  3. In another pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the rice and a pinch of salt. Reduce the heat, cover, and cook until the rice is done (about 15-20 minutes). Set aside.
  4. In a pan, heat mustard oil over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  5. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  6. Add the chopped tomatoes, green chilies, garam masala, coriander powder, and red chili powder. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the spices are well incorporated.
  7. Add the chopped vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes. Add a little water if needed and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  8. In a serving plate, arrange the cooked rice, lentils, and vegetables. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  9. Serve the Mithila Thali with pickles and yogurt on the side.
