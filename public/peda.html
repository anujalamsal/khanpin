Peda Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1.5 - 2 cups milk powder
  2. 1 tin sweetened condensed milk (400gm)
  3. 0.25 cup ghee or butter
  4. A big pinch of cardamom powder
  5. 0.25 cup nuts (optional)
  6. 0.25 cup desiccated coconut (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Take a non-stick pan and add in the milk powder.
  2. Add in condensed milk and give a quick stir.
  3. Add in melted ghee or butter. If adding nuts or desiccated coconut, add them as well. Mix well.
  4. Keep it on the stovetop. Keep stirring continuously under medium flame.
  5. At one stage, after about 7-8 minutes, you can see the mixture starts leaving the bottom.
  6. Add in cardamom powder, mix well, and switch off the stove. Do not overcook it. Overcooking will turn pedas chewy. Transfer it to another bowl.
  7. Wait till the peda mixture is warm. Take gooseberry-sized peda mixture and shape it into pedas. If it is sticking to the palm and you are not able to shape the pedas, that means it is slightly undercooked. Keep it on the stovetop again and stir it for 1 or 2 minutes more.
  8. Once the pedas are done, decorate them with nuts and rest them for 4-5 hours. If you taste it before resting time, it will be sticky.
