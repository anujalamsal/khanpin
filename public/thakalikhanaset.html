Thakali khana set Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups Basmati rice
  2. 1 cup black lentils (urad dal)
  3. 200g mutton or chicken, cut into pieces
  4. 1 onion, finely chopped
  5. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  6. 1/2 cup spinach, chopped
  7. 1/2 cup radish pickle (mula ko achar)
  8. 1/2 cup tomato pickle (golbheda ko achar)
  9. 1/4 cup ghee
  10. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  11. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  12. 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  13. 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  14. 2 green chilies, chopped
  15. Salt to taste
  16. Fresh cilantro for garnish
