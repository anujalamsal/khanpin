Taruwa Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 500g tarul (taro root), peeled and diced
  2. 1 large onion, finely chopped
  3. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  4. 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 1-inch ginger, grated
  6. 2 green chilies, slit
  7. 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  8. 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  9. 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  10. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  11. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  12. 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  13. Salt to taste
  14. 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  15. Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Procedures:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds.
  2. Once the seeds start to splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and slit green chilies. Sauté for another couple of minutes.
  4. Now, add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  5. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for a minute.
  6. Add diced tarul (taro root) to the pan and mix until the masala coats the tarul pieces evenly.
  7. Cover the pan and cook the tarul over low heat until they are tender, stirring occasionally. It may take around 15-20 minutes.
  8. Once the tarul is cooked, sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice or roti. Enjoy your delicious Tarul Curry!
