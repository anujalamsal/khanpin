Fapark ko guldu Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups buckwheat seeds
  2. Water for soaking
  3. Salt to taste

Procedures:

  1. Rinse the buckwheat seeds and soak them in water for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain and rinse the buckwheat seeds again.
  3. Grind the buckwheat seeds into a fine flour using a grinder or blender.
  4. Mix the buckwheat flour with salt and water to form a dough.
  5. Knead the dough for 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and pliable.
  6. Divide the dough into small balls and flatten them slightly into disk shapes.
  7. Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  8. Cook the Fapar ko Guldu for 2-3 minutes on each side, until they are golden brown and crispy.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite Nepali dishes!
