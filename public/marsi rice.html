Marsi rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups Marsee rice (or any red rice variety)
  2. 4 cups water
  3. 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Rinse the Marsee rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the rinsed rice and salt (if using) to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let the rice simmer for about 30-35 minutes, or until the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.
  5. Once cooked, remove the pot from the heat and let it sit, covered, for about 5-10 minutes.
  6. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
  7. Serve the Marsee rice warm, ideally with a side of curry or vegetable dish.
