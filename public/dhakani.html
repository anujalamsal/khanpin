Dhakani Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Split red lentils (masoor dal)
  2. Split black gram (urad dal)
  3. Rice
  4. Water
  5. Salt
  6. Ghee or oil
  7. Chopped onions
  8. Chopped garlic
  9. Chopped ginger
  10. Red chili powder
  11. Turmeric powder
  12. Garam masala powder

Procedures:

  1. Rinse the lentils and rice together and soak them in water for at least 4 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind the mixture into a smooth paste.
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a pan and sauté the onions, garlic, and ginger until golden brown.
  4. Add the red chili powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala powder and sauté for another minute.
  5. Add the ground lentil and rice mixture to the pan and stir well.
  6. Cook the mixture until it thickens and the dhakani is ready to serve.
