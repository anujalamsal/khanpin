Chyang Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Rice
  2. Millet
  3. Barley
  4. Wheat
  5. Yeast balls (or Koji-kin)
  6. Ginger
  7. Water
  8. Windsor Ale yeast
  9. Lactobacillus bacteria
  10. Pearl barley
  11. Dark wheat

Procedures:

  1. Mix shredded store-bought organic ginger with barley flour and water, form into cakes and proof for 3 days under a damp cloth.
  2. Steam some rice, add Koji-kin, and make a moto starter as for sake.
  3. Mix mold-coated rice with Windsor Ale yeast, known for its estery, fruity contribution to a brew.
  4. After three days, add a culture of two strains of lactobacillus bacteria and let it ferment for another day.
  5. The main mash consists of malted barley, to which is added pearl barley, millet, rice, and dark wheat, all of which had been boiled for 20 minutes in a ginger-root tea.
  6. Mash for two hours and cool to room temperature.
  7. Add the moto starter to the mash soup with all the grains, and ferment for three days.
  8. Strain and bottle the Chyang, and keep refrigerated.
