Kinema Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups soybeans
  2. 2 tablespoons turmeric powder
  3. Water for soaking and boiling
  4. 1-2 tablespoons salt
  5. 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 1-inch ginger, grated
  7. 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  8. 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  9. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  10. 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional)
  11. Mustard oil for cooking

Procedures:

  1. Soak the soybeans in water overnight or for at least 8 hours.
  2. Drain the soaked soybeans and grind them into a coarse paste using a food processor or grinder.
  3. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add turmeric powder and the ground soybean paste.
  4. Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring continuously to prevent sticking, for about 30-40 minutes or until the mixture thickens and becomes smooth.
  5. Add salt to taste and continue cooking for another 10-15 minutes.
  6. In a separate pan, heat mustard oil. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies. Saute until fragrant.
  7. Add cumin powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder to the pan. Cook for another minute.
  8. Transfer the cooked spice mixture to the pot with the soybean mixture. Stir well to combine.
  9. Cook the kinema mixture for another 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, to allow the flavors to meld together.
  10. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature before serving.
  11. Serve kinema as a side dish or use it as an ingredient in other recipes. Enjoy!
