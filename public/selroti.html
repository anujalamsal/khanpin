Selroti Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups rice flour
  2. 1 cup water
  3. 1/2 cup sugar
  4. 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  5. 1/4 tsp baking powder
  6. Oil for frying

Procedures:

  1. In a bowl, mix rice flour, sugar, cardamom powder, and baking powder.
  2. Add water gradually and mix to form a smooth batter. It should be thick yet pourable.
  3. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
  4. Pour the batter into a piping bag or squeeze bottle with a small hole.
  5. In a circular motion, pipe the batter into the hot oil to form a round, donut-like shape.
  6. Fry until golden brown on both sides, turning once to ensure even cooking.
  7. Remove from oil and place on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve hot as a snack or dessert.
