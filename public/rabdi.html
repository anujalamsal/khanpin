Rabdi

Ingredients:

  1. 1 liter full-fat milk
  2. 1/2 cup sugar
  3. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  4. 1 tablespoon chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews)
  5. 1 tablespoon chopped dried fruits (raisins)

Procedures:

  1. Pour the milk into a heavy-bottomed pan and bring it to a boil over medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent it from sticking to the bottom of the pan.
  2. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the milk, stirring frequently, until it reduces to about half its original volume. This process can take 1 to 1.5 hours.
  3. Add sugar, cardamom powder, chopped nuts, and dried fruits to the reduced milk. Mix well.
  4. Continue to simmer the mixture for another 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens to a rabdi consistency.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature. Rabdi can be served warm or chilled.
