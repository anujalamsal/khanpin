Momo Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, bell peppers, etc.)
  3. 1 small onion, finely chopped
  4. 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  5. 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  6. 1 tsp soy sauce
  7. 1 tsp sesame oil
  8. Salt and pepper to taste
  9. Water for kneading

Procedures:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour with water to make a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the filling by saut√©ing the onions, garlic, and ginger in sesame oil until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are slightly tender. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them out into thin circles.
  5. Place a spoonful of the vegetable filling in the center of each circle and fold them into momo shapes.
  6. Steam the momos for about 10-15 minutes until cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with a dipping sauce of your choice.
