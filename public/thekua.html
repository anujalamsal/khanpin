Thekua Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups whole wheat flour
  2. 1 cup jaggery (gur), grated
  3. 1/2 cup water
  4. 1/4 cup grated coconut
  5. 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)
  6. 1/4 cup raisins
  7. 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  8. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  9. Ghee or vegetable oil for frying

Procedures:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, grated jaggery, grated coconut, chopped nuts, raisins, fennel seeds, and cardamom powder.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a stiff dough. Cover and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round discs or any desired shape.
  4. Heat ghee or vegetable oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Gently slide the thekuas into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  6. Remove the fried thekuas from the oil and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Allow them to cool completely before serving.
  8. Store the cooled thekuas in an airtight container at room temperature.
