Batuk Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 250 gm black lentils (_kalo maas_)
  2. 3 tablespoons ginger paste
  3. 2 tablespoons garlic paste (optional)
  4. ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  5. ½ liters vegetable oil/sunflower oil
  6. Salt
  7. Optional: finely chopped onions, spring onions, coriander leaves, broad leaf mustard greens (rayo saag), cumin, coriander, black pepper powder, and green chilies

Procedures:

  1. Soak the black lentils in a bowl for overnight or for at least 5-6 hours.
  2. Massage the black lentils using your hand to remove the outer black layer and wash thoroughly with clean water.
  3. Grind the black lentils in a food processor (do not add any water).
  4. Transfer the black lentil paste to a large bowl.
  5. Add salt, turmeric powder, ginger paste, and garlic paste, and mix them together.
  6. Leave the mixture covered for half an hour.
  7. Make sure that the mixture doesn’t stick to the bowl.
