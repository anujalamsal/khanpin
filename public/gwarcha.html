Gwarcha Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Milk
  2. Yogurt
  3. Salt
  4. Water

Procedures:

  1. Boil the milk and let it cool.
  2. Add yogurt to the cooled milk and mix well.
  3. Add salt to taste.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a container and let it ferment for a few hours.
  5. Once fermented, churn the mixture to separate the butter and buttermilk.
  6. Collect the butter and shape it into a ball or any desired shape.
