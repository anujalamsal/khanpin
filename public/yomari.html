Yomari Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups of rice flour
  2. 1 cup of water
  3. 1 cup of coconut, grated
  4. 1 cup of molasses
  5. 1/2 teaspoon of cardamom powder
  6. Pinch of salt

Procedures:

  1. Mix rice flour and water to form a smooth dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small cups.
  3. Fill the cups with a mixture of grated coconut, molasses, cardamom powder, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Seal the cups and shape them into a figure-eight pattern.
  5. Steam the yomaris for about 15-20 minutes.
  6. Serve warm and enjoy!
