Anadi ko kheer Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 cup rice
  2. 4 cups milk
  3. 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  4. 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  5. 1/4 cup mixed nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios), chopped
  6. 1/4 cup raisins
  7. 1 teaspoon cardamom powder
  8. Saffron strands (optional)
  9. Silver leaf (varak) for garnish (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for about 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the rice and set it aside.
  3. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat.
  4. Add the drained rice to the milk and reduce the heat to low.
  5. Cook the rice in the milk, stirring occasionally, until the rice is soft and the milk has thickened (about 30-40 minutes).
  6. Add sugar, ghee, mixed nuts, raisins, cardamom powder, and saffron strands to the kheer.
  7. Cook for another 5-10 minutes, stirring continuously, until the sugar is dissolved and the kheer has reached a thick consistency.
  8. Remove the kheer from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  9. Serve the kheer chilled or at room temperature, garnished with silver leaf if desired. Enjoy the creamy and delicious Anadi ko Kheer!
