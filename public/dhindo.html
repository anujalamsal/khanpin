Dhido Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Water: 2 cups
  2. Buckwheat or millet flour: 1 cup
  3. Salt: to taste

Procedures:

  1. In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  2. Gradually add the buckwheat or millet flour into the boiling water, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook and stir the mixture until it thickens and becomes smooth, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Add salt to taste and continue stirring for another minute.
  5. Remove from heat and serve hot.
