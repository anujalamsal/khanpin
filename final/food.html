Chatamari

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups rice flour
  2. 1 cup water
  3. 1/2 lb ground meat (chicken, lamb, or beef)
  4. 1 onion, finely chopped
  5. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  6. 1/2 cup green peas
  7. 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  8. 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  9. 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Oil for cooking

Procedures:

  1. Mix rice flour and water to make a smooth batter. Let it sit for 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix ground meat, onions, tomatoes, green peas, green chilies, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and salt.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and pour a ladle of batter to make a thin pancake.
  4. Spread the meat mixture on top of the pancake and cook until the bottom is golden brown.
  5. Flip the chatamari and cook the other side until meat is cooked through.
  6. Repeat for the remaining batter and meat mixture.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious chatamari!
