Bhakka Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups rice flour
  2. Water (as needed)
  3. 1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Wash the millet thoroughly and drain.
  2. Cook the millet with water until it's soft and fully cooked.
  3. Allow the cooked millet to cool to room temperature.
  4. Add the starter culture to the cooled millet and mix well.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a container, cover it, and let it ferment at room temperature for 2-3 days.
  6. After fermentation, the millet is ready to be served.
  7. To serve, fill a tongba vessel with the fermented millet and pour hot water over it. Allow it to sit for a few minutes.
  8. Drink the tongba using a straw, adding more hot water as needed to extract more flavor from the millet.
