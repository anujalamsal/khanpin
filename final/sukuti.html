Sukuti Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 kg of buffalo or goat meat, thinly sliced
  2. 1/2 cup of mustard oil or vegetable oil
  3. 1 tablespoon of cumin seeds
  4. 1 tablespoon of coriander powder
  5. 1 tablespoon of garlic paste
  6. 1 tablespoon of ginger paste
  7. 1 tablespoon of red chili powder
  8. 1 tablespoon of turmeric powder
  9. 1 tablespoon of salt
  10. 2 cups of water

Procedures:

  1. Wash the meat and pat it dry with a clean cloth.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the cumin seeds until they are fragrant.
  3. Add the coriander powder, garlic paste, ginger paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add the meat and stir fry for 5-7 minutes or until the meat is coated with the spices.
  5. Add 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 1-2 hours or until the meat is tender and the water has evaporated.
  7. Spread the meat on a tray and let it dry in the sun for 2-3 days or until it is completely dry and crispy.
  8. Store the sukuti in an airtight container and use it as a snack or add it to other dishes for extra flavor.
