Gundruk Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Leafy vegetables (mustard greens, radish leaves, cauliflower leaves, etc.)
  2. Salt
  3. Water

Procedures:

  1. Ferment the leafy vegetables and/or roots for a week or more.
  2. Dry the sour vegetables in the sun.
  3. Re-hydrate the dried leaves briefly in hot water before using.
  4. Use the re-hydrated leaves in salads or "pickles" (i.e. achar), or add dry leaves directly to soups.
