Chilla Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup rava / sooji / semolina, coarse
  2. ½ cup curd / yogurt
  3. ½ tsp salt
  4. 1 cup water
  5. 2 tbsp onion, finely chopped
  6. 2 tbsp tomato, finely chopped
  7. 1 green chilli, finely chopped
  8. ½ tsp ginger paste
  9. 2 tbsp coriander leaves, finely chopped
  10. Oil for roasting chilla

Procedures:

  1. Firstly, in a large bowl take 1 cup rava and ½ cup curd.
  2. Also add ½ tsp salt and ¾ cup water.
  3. With the help of whisk, mix well without forming any lumps.
  4. Rest for 20 minutes or till rava swells completely.
