Kachila Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 500g minced buffalo meat (or any minced meat of choice)
  2. 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  3. 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  4. 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  5. 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  6. 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  8. 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  10. Salt to taste
  11. 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, finely chopped
  12. 1 onion, finely chopped
  13. Juice of 1 lemon

Procedures:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the minced meat, ginger paste, garlic paste, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground black pepper, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt.
  2. Mix thoroughly until all the spices are well incorporated into the meat.
  3. Heat the mustard oil in a pan until it reaches its smoking point, then let it cool slightly.
  4. Pour the heated mustard oil over the spiced meat mixture and mix well.
  5. Add the finely chopped cilantro, onion, and lemon juice to the meat mixture. Mix thoroughly.
  6. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let the mixture marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  7. Once marinated, shape the meat mixture into small patties or balls.
  8. In a non-stick pan, cook the patties over medium heat until they are browned and cooked through, turning occasionally.
