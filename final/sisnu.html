Sisnu Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 bunch of stinging nettle leaves (sisne ko saag)
  2. 2 medium-sized potatoes, peeled and diced
  3. 1 medium-sized onion, chopped
  4. 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  5. 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
  6. 1 teaspoon of salt
  7. 2 tablespoons of mustard oil or vegetable oil
  8. 2 cups of water
  9. Optional: 1/2 teaspoon of cumin seeds, 1/2 teaspoon of coriander powder

Procedures:

  1. Wash the stinging nettle leaves thoroughly and drain the water.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the chopped onion and minced garlic until the onion is translucent.
  3. Add the diced potatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add the stinging nettle leaves and mix well.
  5. Add 2 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the potatoes are cooked.
  7. Optional: Add cumin seeds and coriander powder for extra flavor.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.
