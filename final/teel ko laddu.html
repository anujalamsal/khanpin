Teel ko laddu Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. ⅓ cup sesame seeds (hulled or unhulled white sesame seeds)
  2. ¼ cup peanuts (raw peanuts)
  3. 1 cup jaggery (gud or gur)
  4. Desiccated coconut (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Heat a kadai or pan and add ⅓ cup sesame seeds to it. Roast the sesame seeds on low heat, stirring at intervals, until they pop and change color. Do not brown them.
  2. Remove the roasted sesame seeds and keep them aside on a plate.
  3. Add ¼ cup peanuts to the same pan and dry roast them on low heat till they become crunchy and get a few black spots or blisters on them. Stir often.
  4. Remove the peanuts and allow them to cool. If desired, remove the peanut skins by rubbing them between your palms.
  5. Cook the jaggery to a soft ball stage or hard ball stage, depending on the desired texture of the laddu.
  6. Mix the roasted sesame seeds, peanuts, and jaggery syrup together.
  7. Add desiccated coconut if desired.
  8. Shape the mixture into small balls or laddu.
  9. Store the Teel Ko Laddu in an airtight container and enjoy!
